About

We are building a modular and cloud based ERP solution to meet today's business and technological requirements.

Project4Spark is an open source, modular and cloud based ERP being developed for small and medium enterprises.

Open Source

Project4Spark is being developed using open source technologies (Python, Django, Postgresql, ...) and will be open source.

Modular and Extensible

Project4Spark is modular and can be extensible by installing additional apps or plugins. You pay only for what you use.

Cloud Based

Project4Spark is a cloud based solution with community and commercial versions.
