About

Project4Spark is an open source, modular and cloud based ERP solution being developed for small and medium enterprises.

Using open source technologies, Project4Spark aims to meet today's business and technological requirements that come with the digital economy.

In accordance with our vision, we are working to combine the classical ERP modules and processes with the new technologies and concepts.

You can see our progress on this page.
